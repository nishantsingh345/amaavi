The Amaavi Philosophy

Our story dates back to the time our world came into existence.
Travel back in time with us.

Centuries before the first city was built, eons before the first book was written, and millennia before humans started using ‘#wanderlust’ : our ancestors developed a single most pivotal trait that aided our evolution as a species. That trait was migration - simply put, the urge to travel and discover new lands. Scientists believe that this was a drive so important, that, if not for the yearning we developed for it, our species would have almost certainly perished. Travel has led to discovery, of the world around us, and of ourselves. It presents us with the unique opportunity to experience cultures, its people, their ways of life, and everything that is good and bountiful on our planet - below, at, and above sea level. It takes us out of our comfort zones, and expands our worldview, pushing us to grow, to be open and accepting, tolerant and grateful for the gift of life.

Travel is immersive, in that, it changes and moulds perspectives - and when one comes out on the other side of a journey, they are changed as people, for the better. Ergo, travel is as external as it is internal, and the same can be said for fewer things. We are connected as a people today, only because someone, somewhere, way before our time, took the bold step of disregarding norms and preconceived notions and set out to discover what the world beyond the known world was. We have journeyed across seas, voyaged across oceans, and the thirst for exploration and discovery has now propelled us into the cosmos. That is the power of travel, that continually pushes the human race forward. If a timeline were to be drawn from the beginning of time and into the future, travel would be constant, and because of it, change.

Ashwath Vyas - CEO

Ashwath Vyas S is the Founder & CEO of Amaavi Experiences Pvt Ltd. Amaavi was founded in August, 2017 with the mission to make travel a hassle free and meaningful experience for people. Ashwath's tryst with travel began in 2011 when he was selected to intern with Grand Group Corporation, Ningbo, China. New to the country, people, food and language, the experience of staying away from home for 4 months was a transformational experience for him. He came back and soon started working with Worldview Education - an experiential learning company that designed education travel programs for high school students. During the 4 years that he spent there, he managed partnerships with universities such as Harvard, MIT, Columbia, Oxford, Yale among others and think tanks and government organisations. Through these partnerships, he helped to design travel elements around history, culture, international diplomacy, university immersions and personal development. Ashwath quit Worldview to work in the Real Estate space with Giridhari Homes heading Marketing & Sales for one year before finally taking time off to reflect and realise that his passion is to travel and meet people. As a student in CBIT, where he studied Civil Engineering, he was actively part of the youth organisation - AIESEC for 3 years, where he had the chance to meet and connect with young people from over 50 countries. This was the genesis of his passion.
